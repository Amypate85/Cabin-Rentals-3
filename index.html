ALL CABINS FEATURE HOT TUBS, FIREPLACES, AND FREE WI-FI

Our Rainier lodging options offer eight beautiful vacation rental homes nestled in the shadow of Mt. Rainier, just outside the year-round Paradise entrance to Mt. Rainier National Park in Ashford, WA. Located about two hours from both Seattle and Portland, our cabins comfortably accommodate groups of all sizes. We have four dog-friendly cabins, three creekside cabins, and one cabin ideal for large groups, family reunions, or company gatherings. In addition, all of our Mt. Rainier cabins have hot tubs! Whether you're seeking a romantic weekend, a quiet escape, a base camp for outdoor adventures like hiking, or a fun-filled family getaway, a Three Bears Cabins property is sure to become your new favorite mountain retreat!
